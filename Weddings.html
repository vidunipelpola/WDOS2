Weddings


Enjoy your special day with the one you love at Hotel . Our romantic atmosphere and convenience is bound to make your day go beautifully. Our sustainable facilities will also ensure your wedding has minimal waste left over, heightening your experience.


Meals

Make your special day extra special for you and your loved ones with our delectable food and drinks that are sure to bring satisfaction to all. We offer many cuisines from all around the world, for appetizers, main courses, and desserts.


Extras

Floral Arrangements


We will beautify your wedding with our floral arrangements that are done intricately, to add romance and incredible detail to the venue of your special event. You can choose to have these flowers preserved, or they will be upcycled or reused to minimize waste.


Decorations


We provide eco-friendly, sustainable decorations for your wedding, here at Hotel , to make your day a no-waste day! Decorations can be purchased, and will be reused if not

Planning


Our wedding planners will ensure that your event reflects your personal style, runs smoothly and economically


Details

All weddings planned by us come with all the extras mentioned above, and the schedule for the venue (beach or reception hall) is as follows;


  • Daytime Weddings - 7:00 - 17:00
  • Evening Weddings - 16:00 - 23:30

All accomodations will be made on availability, as per the hotel's policy
